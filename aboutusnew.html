Tentang Bulan Cafe are warm, welcoming places that have become essential to contemporary social and cultural life because they provide the ideal setting for people to congregate, unwind, or work. Along with a delectable assortment of light snacks, pastries, or even full meals, they usually serve a broad range of beverages, including freshly brewed coffee, fragrant teas, and cool, refreshing drinks. Tentang Bulan Cafés create a welcoming environment where people can have meaningful conversations, catch up with friends, or just take a moment for themselves thanks to their cosy ambiance, calming background music, and attractive interior design. Many cafés provide free Wi-Fi and peaceful areas in addition to food and beverages, which makes them perfect for professionals, freelancers, and students.





Our Coffee: Our baristas are extremely knowledgeable about the best coffee beans, which are roasted in Cape Coral, and we serve a variety of espresso coffees made from them. They will make you the ideal cup of coffee thanks to their extensive technical expertise and love for creating delicious coffee.


Our Food: To give you fresher, healthier, and incredibly delicious meals, we only use natural, straightforward ingredients that are sourced locally whenever possible. We serve international cuisine, have a well-stocked pastry cabinet, and provide vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options.


Tentang Bulan events & Private functions: We have monthly dinners, chef's tasting dinners, and other events at Tentang Bulan Cafe Events & Private Functions. We also like to host your special events, such as office parties,birthday celebrations, anniversaries, and baby showers.
